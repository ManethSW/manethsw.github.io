About

Promoting and bringing light to the Ceylon Tea Industry that has been established since the 1940s. Ceylon Tea has marked its' place in the world for its finest quality tea and continuing to improve its' stanard along the way. In the midst of all the changes taking place in the tea industry, Ceylon Tea Company is keeping its foothold by innovating and improving its' production methods. In 1945, Ceylon Tea was introduced to the world as a fine quality tea. Since then, it has consistently set the bar for quality. Ceylon Tea has a robust history and continues to be a leader in the industry.
